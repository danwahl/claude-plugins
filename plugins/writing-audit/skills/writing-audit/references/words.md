## Tier 3: Flag by density (common, but overused in aggregate)

enhance, innovative/innovation, significant(ly), furthermore, moreover, additionally, however, therefore, consequently, nonetheless, transform(ative), demonstrate(s), establish, compelling, ensure, emerge, reinforce, profound, prominent, paramount, tangible, discover.

## Adverbs that rarely earn their place

genuinely, truly, fundamentally, inherently, inevitably, importantly, crucially, significantly, interestingly, deeply, simply, actually, really, just, literally, honestly, meticulously, seamlessly.

## Transitions that stack up (flag when >1 per 1k words)
